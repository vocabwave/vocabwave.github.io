What is VocabWave?

VocabWave is an online platform to improve vocabulary. Users can create lists, practice with tools, and customize their learning. Emphasizing privacy, VocabWave stores no data on its servers; everything is saved locally in the user's browser, ensuring a secure and private learning experience.

Use with browser

VocabWave offers a dedicated Android app and is accessible on all devices via browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and others. Chrome users can add VocabWave to their home screen for quick access. Our platform is compatible with Android, iOS, Windows, Linux, and utilizes browser local storage, making it versatile for internet-enabled devices.
